Welcome to Vidya Vikas Institute Of Engineering and Technology

The Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology is one of the top 10 engineering colleges in Mysore, Karnataka. Affiliated to VTU Mysore, our college was founded in 1997 and has now grown rapidly into one of the best engineering colleges in Karnataka. Our courses include BE., MBA, and MCA. Counted as one of the top Engineering colleges in Karnataka for its placement opportunities, we cater to 3000 students and staff members in an environment that is rich in cultural and linguistic diversity.

Mission of the Institute

  • To offer highest Professional and Academic Standards in terms of Personal growth and satisfaction.
  • To provide students a platform where independent learning and scientific study are encouraged with emphasis on latest engineering techniques.
  • To encourage students to implement applications of Engineering with a focus on societal needs for the betterment of communities.
  • To empower students with vast technical and life skills to raise their stakes of getting placements in top reputed companies.
  • To create a benchmark in the areas of Research, Education and Public Outreach.

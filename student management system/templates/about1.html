About Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology

Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology is renowned for nurturing technical expertise, fostering innovation, and preparing students for the dynamic world of engineering and technology. Our institution focuses on holistic development through cutting-edge research, hands-on projects, and industry collaborations.

Our Achievements

Our institute takes pride in its significant accomplishments in the engineering and technology fields.

  • Ranked among the Top 5 Engineering Institutes in Karnataka
  • Best Infrastructure Award in 2023 for state-of-the-art labs and facilities
  • 95% Placement Rate with top-tier companies like Infosys, Wipro, and TCS
  • Winner of National Robotics Championship - 2022

Faculty Excellence

Our faculty consists of experienced engineers, researchers, and industry professionals dedicated to delivering quality education. They guide students to excel both academically and practically.

  • 50+ PhD Scholars leading innovation in various engineering fields
  • Faculty members have published 200+ research papers in top international journals
  • Active collaboration with global universities and companies like Google and Intel

Campus Life

At Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology, campus life is a perfect blend of academic rigor and extracurricular activities that foster holistic growth. The campus is designed to offer students a vibrant and inclusive environment where they can thrive both in and out of the classroom.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our campus boasts cutting-edge facilities designed to support a wide range of academic and co-curricular activities.

  • Advanced Engineering Labs
  • Smart Classrooms
  • Research Centers in AI, Robotics, and Renewable Energy

Cultural and Technical Fests

We host cultural fest **Vidyotsava** and technical fest **Technovation**, providing platforms for students to showcase their talent and innovations.

  • Vidyotsava: A grand celebration of arts, music, and culture
  • Technovation: Annual technology fest featuring hackathons and expert talks

Sports and Fitness

Our campus includes top-notch sports facilities where students can unwind, compete, and stay fit.

  • Cricket Ground, Football Field, and Basketball Courts
  • Gym and Yoga Center for health and wellness
  • Annual Sports Meet to encourage healthy competition

Clubs and Societies

Our student-run clubs and societies focus on academics, social service, and artistic talents.
